Chocolate Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

I dunno about this one. It sounds like it could work but honestly, I haven't tried it so it's really up to you. Give it a go, hopefully it tastes nice.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet hot chocolate powder mix
  • 1 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk (semi-skimmed or whole as preferred)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Pour the hot chocolate powder into blender
  2. Add youghurt, milk, and peanut butter
  3. Add ice (optional)
  4. Blend until well mixed
  5. Serve and enjoy
