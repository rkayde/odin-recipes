Strawberry Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

Who knew strawberries and peanut butter could co-exist so nicely?

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain youghurt
  • 1/4 cup milk (semi-skimmed or whole is fine)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Slice the strawberries and place into blender
  2. Add yoghurt, milk, and peanut butter
  3. Add ice (optional)
  4. Blend until desired consistency
  5. Serve and enjoy
