Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

A tasty protein-filled peanut butter and banana smoothie recipe. Perfect for a post-workout energy boost or for relaxing in the sun on a warm summer's day.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana broken into bits
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of peanut buuter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Crushed ice

Steps

  1. Break the banana into chunks and place into blender
  2. Add milk, peanut butter, and honey
  3. Add ice (optional)
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Serve and enjoy
